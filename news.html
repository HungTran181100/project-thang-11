The Legend of Zelda™: Echoes of Wisdom is out now!

Available now

A darkness has seeped into the kingdom of Hyrule. As ordinary folks go about their daily lives, mysterious rifts are tearing through the lands and swallowing up entire areas—along with the people! Even a heroic swordsman named Link has disappeared into a rift—without him, who is to save the day?!

NintendoGameSpaces brings you Princess Zelda from Nintendo - The Legend of Zelda™!

Princess Zelda - armed with a powerful staff called Tri Rod given by her companion named Tri, exploring the vast, expansive lands of Hyrule to mend the rift-filled kingdom. However, in The Legend of Zelda™: Echoes of Wisdom game, you don’t just rely on your strength and courage to solve puzzles and take down enemies. Zelda has more… creative methods.

NintendoGameSpaces brings you a fascinating journey from Nintendo - The Legend of Zelda™

Echoes - The power of Wisdom

After acquiring the Tri Rod, Zelda can use it to create “echoes,” imitations of things that she finds as she explores Hyrule. Beds, pots, monsters, trampolines—you can obtain a plethora of different echoes in this game. The variety is endless! How you approach obstacles depends on you and the echoes you’ve obtained. For example, let’s look at two ways of going through a problem:

Possible scenario #1: Hey, let’s avoid the enemies! We can create a trampoline echo to reach a higher ledge.

Possible scenario #2: Hey, let’s defeat the enemies! We can jump on a trampoline echo while holding a Crow echo to glide behind enemies, and then use a Ignizol echo to take them out all at once.

Just remember that every encounter—whether it’s with an enemy or environmental puzzle—is like its own little mini-puzzle. It’s up to you how you want to play the game!

The Dark Dungeons

Zelda’s investigation of the rifts will lead to the Still World—a place where you’ll take on dungeons filled with enemies, puzzles, and monstrous bosses. Just remember to experiment with different echoes when approaching an obstacle.

Each Dungeon will resides different challenges and bosses, so be prepared by obtaining powerful echoes and brewing effective smoothies! As you progress through the first dungeon, Zelda will also gaining new abilites.

The Bond and the Sword

In addition to creating echoes, Zelda also has a few more powerful abilities. Use Bind to make objects follow Zelda’s movements, or Reverse Bond to follow an object’s movements. You can also use a mysterious sword to transform into the powerful swordfighter form (but you’ll revert forms after a meter runs out—so plan ahead!).

Visit the NintendoGameSpaces wikipage of The Legend of Zelda™: Echoes of Wisdom for more tips and tricks on these special abilities!

A lively Hyrule

Hyrule is a vast kingdom, and there’s a lot to discover. There are eccentric characters, side quests, minigames, outfits, accessories, delicious smoothies, and more. As you explore different regions, you’ll help locals, tackle dungeons, learn new echoes, and then it's for your wisdom to decide!
